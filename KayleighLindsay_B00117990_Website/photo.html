I am an amateur photographer; I have always had an interest in photography but have not had the money or resources to pursue it until college.

I use various Nikon DSLR camera models in college to take my shots; mainly D3000 and D60 models. Our college has a photography studio on campus that students are free to utilise for their assignments. There are several overhead softboxes and other lights in the studio to use, along with flash triggers and gels and reflectors. I used these in my product photography shots as part of my assignment, and also used them in headshots.

While I am only starting photography, I am enjoying it greatly! I am glad my college has these modules as I have always had an interest in this area but never had the supplies to try it out.
