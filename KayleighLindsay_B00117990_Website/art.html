This is the artworks page of my site! Here, you can find a collecton of artwork I have done over a long period of time. These are completely unrelated to college; all done in my free time as part of my hobby!

I have taken up digital artwork in recent years, and it is now my main medium of art. I still do traditional work from time to time, but fnd digital art more satisfying and easier.

For digital work, I use a Wacom Intuos tablet along with the programme Paint Tool SAI, and when I am away from home I use an app called IbisPaint X on my phone.

For traditional artwork, I mainly use Faber Castell products such as their Polychromos, graphite pencils and watercolour pencils. I also use Fineliners and white gel pens often.
