This is the media page of my website!

Here, you can find a variety of works I have done throughout my course that are media based works.

For one of my assignments, I had to create a 3 minute video in a group! This meant finding actors, finding locations, organising shooting times, shooting the video over a period of time. And afterwards, we had to individually edit our videos and present them to our class and lecturer.

For another one of my assignments, I had to create an interactive text based game on a site called Twine. Using variables, I made mine interactive by having items the character could pick up and use, and different outcomes depending on the relationship you build with the secondary character!
