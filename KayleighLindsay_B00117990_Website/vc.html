This is the Visual Creativity page of my site. Here, you can find a collection of sketches and artworks relating to this module. These sketches were all done by hand using references and primary research and in an A3 sketchbook. We did a combination of collage work and sketching in our sketchbooks.

We had to explore the theme "Mechanic Organic" and make our own theme within it. I decided to go with the theme "Decay and Replication" to show that while organic things die, mechanic things are constantly developing and will replace us in certain ways.

It's purpose was to serve as a health campaign to bring awareness to the developing importance of technology in our health.

We had to go through a variety of visual and experimentation in order to develop our final piece.
