Welcome to my ePortfolio!

My name is Kayleigh Lindsay, and I am a student currently in my first year studying Creative Digital Media in Technological University Dublin.

I have a passion for creativity; I have spent many years drawing and crafting and have always had an interest in creating. I have won several art awards and was even given a scholarship for a summer course in the National College of Art and Design.

Throughout my time in my course, I have had the opportunity to create many kinds of work in different fields, such as photography, filmmaking, design, visual communication and website development. Come take a look!
